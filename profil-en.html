Results, Not Slides.

Georgiana Bonas EMBA — Founder & Managing Director

Georgiana Bonas brings over 15 years of experience in industrial transformation, supply chain management and global trade. She guides companies across the DACH region and internationally through complex restructurings, ERP transformations and operational realignments.

With strategic clarity and a deep understanding of industrial value chains, she identifies where costs accumulate, decisions stall and performance is lost — and creates the foundation for sustainable results there.

Her approach: no presentations, no theory — but solutions that work, are measurable and lasting. From analysis to implementation, she brings structure to organisations, establishes clear accountability and ensures that decisions get made.
