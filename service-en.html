Services

Four Models.
One Goal: Your Results.

Tailored consulting formats for industrial companies — from a rapid situation assessment to long-term transformation support.

Our Models

How do we work together?

Orientation

Clarity Before Action

A compact engagement that gives you rapid orientation. We listen, analyse the situation and provide you with a clear picture — what it is, what it needs, what makes sense next.

  • Initial conversation and situation analysis (2–4 hrs)
  • Diagnostic workshop on-site or remote
  • Written situation assessment and options for action
  • Prioritisation matrix and initial recommendations
  • Follow-up meeting after 4 weeks
Enquire →

Stabilization

Regaining Control

When pressure builds, processes begin to falter or crises loom: we step in, create stability and give you back control. Swiftly, clearly, decisively.

  • Crisis diagnosis and immediate measures
  • Stakeholder communication and expectation management
  • Process stabilization and quick wins
  • Building interim structures and routines
  • Weekly steering meetings (4–12 weeks)
  • Final report and handover planning
Enquire →

Transformation

Shaping the Future

Deep-rooted change requires time, trust and a clear architecture. We accompany you through the entire transformation process — from vision to sustainable embedding.

  • Strategic target picture development and roadmap
  • Process and organisational redesign
  • Digital transformation and system integration
  • Change management and employee involvement
  • Regular review cycles and adjustments
  • Knowledge transfer and internal capability development
Enquire →

Executive Retainer

Support for Leadership

As a continuous strategic partner for executives: a reliable sparring partner who knows your situation, thinks critically and operates at eye level — when you need it.

  • Monthly 1:1 strategy sessions (2–4 hrs)
  • Ad-hoc availability for urgent matters
  • Review of key decisions and documents
  • Network access and external perspective
  • Confidential support for leadership decisions
  • Quarterly retrospective and outlook
