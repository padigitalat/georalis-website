2. Data Collected & Purposes

Assessment Form
Data from the assessment form (industry, problem area, contact details) is used exclusively to process your enquiry. Legal basis: Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR.

When you visit the website, Cloudflare, Inc. automatically stores log data (IP address, browser, operating system, time, pages accessed). This data is not combined with other data sources. Legal basis: Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR. Cloudflare acts as a data processor pursuant to Art. 28 GDPR.
