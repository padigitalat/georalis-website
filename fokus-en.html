Industrial Transformation

ERP Transformation · Digitalization · Change Management

Transformation projects rarely fail due to technology — they fail because of structures, silo thinking and inadequate change management. We guide industrial companies through ERP transformations, digitalization initiatives and operational realignments with a clear focus on results.

From process mapping through system selection to go-live support: we ensure that your investment in transformation delivers measurable ROI — in mechanical engineering, automotive supply chains, electronics manufacturing and plant construction.

  • Process analysis and modelling for manufacturing & production
  • Digital maturity assessment and roadmap development
  • Change management and employee development in industry
  • KPI systems and performance tracking for industrial processes
  • Post-merger integration and operational realignment

    Operational Excellence · Lean Manufacturing · KPI Management

    Inefficient production processes, excessive inventory levels, simultaneous material shortages, missing KPI structures: these problems cost money every single day. We analyse your production, identify waste and implement sustainable improvements.

    From Lean Manufacturing and Six Sigma to KPI-based management — we measurably increase your operational excellence. The result: 30–60% cost reduction in focused areas, faster decisions, clear governance structures.

    • Production optimization: Lean, Six Sigma, Value Stream Mapping

    • KPI dashboard and performance management for production & manufacturing
    • Breaking down silos: collaboration between procurement, production and logistics

    Global Trade & Customs Optimization

    Optimize Customs Costs · Preferential Origin · Trade Compliance

    Incorrect customs tariff numbers, unused free trade agreements, missing trade compliance — these errors cost industrial companies money every day and carry significant risks. We make your global trade processes transparent, compliant and cost-efficient.

    From TARIC analysis through Incoterms optimization to CBAM/EUDR compliance: we integrate customs and global trade as a strategic lever in your supply chain — rather than treating it as a box-ticking exercise.

    • Optimize customs costs: TARIC analysis, customs tariff number optimization
    • Incoterms & delivery structures: EXW vs. DDP, regaining cost control
    • Preferential origin: leverage free trade agreements, calculate origin
    • Trade compliance: export controls, CBAM, EUDR, customs audit preparation
    • System integration: SAP GTS, Oracle GTM, AEB — use strategically, not just manage
    • Building internal compliance structures and AEO certification
